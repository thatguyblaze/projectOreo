What benefits come with a new set of tires?

When you choose Treadz for your tire needs, you aren't just buying rubber. It's all about commitment to your safety. Every full set purchase includes our care package:

  • Precision Balancing for a smooth ride.

We do work with some major insurance providers for towing services. However, coverage varies by policy. Please call us at 423-357-4551, so we can verify if your specific insurer is accepted and handle the process for you.
