Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

At Treadz Tire & Towing ("we", "us", or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our towing and tire services. We believe in transparency and honesty, just like we run our shop.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you call for service or request a quote. This includes:

  • Name and Contact Information
  • Vehicle Information (Year, Make, Model, VIN)
  • Vehicle Location for Dispatch
  • Payment Information (Processed securely via our merchant provider)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to get you back on the road:

  • Dispatching drivers to your exact location.
  • Communicating regarding ETA and service status.
  • Processing payments for services rendered.
  • Maintaining service history for warranties (on tires).

We NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data stays with us.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. While no method is 100% secure, we treat your data with the same care we treat your vehicle.
