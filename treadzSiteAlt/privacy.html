Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

At Treadz Tire & Towing ("we", "us", or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our towing and tire services.


2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you call for service or request a quote. This includes:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Vehicle Location and Description
  • Payment Information (processed securely at the time of service)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of:

  • Disatching drivers to your location.
  • Communicating with you regarding your service status.
  • Processing payments for services rendered.
  • Internal record keeping for warranty and service history.

We NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.


4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
