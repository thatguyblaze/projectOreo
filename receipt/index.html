Our Story

More than just a diner, we're a part of the Mount Carmel community.

For years, the Dairy Cup has been a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. We pride ourselves on serving up classic American comfort food made with care, from juicy burgers and crispy fries to hearty dinners and, of course, our famous hand-spun shakes and Mayfield ice cream treats.

We believe in good food, fair prices, and friendly service. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a sweet treat after the game, we're always happy to see you. Thanks for being part of the Dairy Cup family!
