About GSP

The Grant Space Program is committed to being the most engaging and user-driven Ro-Space group on Roblox. We focus on creating thrilling experiences like realistic rocket launches, collaborative space exploration missions, and fostering a passionate community.

Founded on January 22, 2024, we continuously strive for innovation and community involvement in all our cosmic endeavors.

The GSP Experience Game

Ambitious Interactive Roleplay

Prepare for an unprecedented Roblox adventure! We are actively developing a massive, interactive roleplaying game focused on the entire lifecycle of space exploration. From designing and launching rockets to managing complex missions and exploring distant worlds, you'll be at the heart of the action.

This evolving universe aims to deliver deep immersion and collaborative gameplay. More details coming soon!
