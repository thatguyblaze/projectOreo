Your Neighborhood Diner

Proudly serving the Mount Carmel community for years.

Welcome to the Dairy Cup! We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot where friends and family come together over classic American comfort food. From our juicy burgers and crispy fries to hearty dinners and hand-spun shakes, everything is made with care.

We believe in simple goodness: delicious food, friendly service, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether it's a quick lunch or a sweet Mayfield ice cream treat, we're happy to be your go-to spot. Thanks for being part of our story!
