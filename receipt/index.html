ABOUT GSP

Our Mission (Est. 1/22/24)

The Grant Space Program is committed to being Roblox's most user-involved and detailed Ro-Space simulation group. We provide exciting experiences centered around rocket missions, ground control, and collaborative space exploration challenges, emphasizing technical detail and teamwork.

We aim to foster a community passionate about spaceflight simulation.

THE GSP EXPERIENCE

Core Gameplay: Command the Launch

Our simulation focuses intensely on the launch and landing phases. Master ground operations and coordinate with your crew in mission control.

Key Features:

  • Ground Ops: Manage power, fuel, systems checks, pad readiness.
  • Mission Control: Monitor telemetry, manage stages, track trajectory, handle anomalies.
  • Team Roles: Fill specialized roles (Flight Director, Engineer, etc.) requiring coordination.

Mission Phases:

1. Pre-Launch
  • Checklists & Procedures
  • Power Systems Activation
  • Propellant Loading Ops
  • Comms & Telemetry Lock
  • Weather & Range Go/No-Go
2. Launch & Ascent/Landing
  • Terminal Count Sequence
  • Liftoff & Performance Monitoring
  • Trajectory Tracking
  • Anomaly Response
  • Staging/Landing Commands

Multiplayer coordination is essential for success.
