Who We Are

The Grant Space Program stands as the most dedicated and user-involved Ro-Space group on Roblox. Our core mission is to provide an exciting, immersive experience for everyone interested in space, from launching complex rocket missions to exploring the vast unknown.

We are a community built on passion and collaboration. Founded on January 22, 2024, we're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in Ro-Space.
