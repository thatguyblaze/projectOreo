About Our Community

Welcome to Extreme Juicers! Our server started somewhat unexpectedly during the peak of COVID. A couple of us had just returned from Hawaii and were stuck in a mandatory two-week quarantine. What began as a way to pass the time with friends quickly grew into something much more.

We've fostered a pretty chill community here. You don't need to be a Minecraft pro to join â€“ just bring your enthusiasm for playing, learning, and having fun! We welcome everyone, whether you're an old friend or someone new joining us online.

Our goal is simply to enjoy the game together, build cool things, and maybe get a little better along the way. We host regular events, encourage collaborative builds, and maintain a friendly atmosphere.

Server Events & Activities

We love hosting events! Our biggest one is Minecraft Masters (MCM), which happens a few times a year and pits players against each other in various challenges designed to test skill, strategy, and creativity. We've had 7 MCM events so far, each with unique themes and champions!

Beyond MCM, we often spin up secondary servers, usually featuring mods or unique challenges and goals to keep things fresh. These have included skyblock challenges, hardcore worlds, and themed modpacks like Create Above & Beyond or RLCraft (for the brave!).

Keep an eye out for other one-off events and games too! Players like ScaryScoop are known for building some truly crazy and inventive games by the end of a season. There's always something happening, from build competitions to PvP tournaments!

Server Lore Archives

Incident: The Chicken Wars

It all began innocuously with the termination of a chicken entity designated Pit. This action precipitated a server-wide escalation of poultry-themed sabotage operations. In response, the "Foxers" unit was formed, deploying Canis Vulpes assets. The opposing faction initiated systematic vulpine eradication protocols. Operative Ramjema acted as an agent provocateur, exacerbating hostilities.

Conflict resolution occurred following the loss of two high-value companion foxes. A ceasefire agreement between Foxer Lead and Panda concluded the Chicken Wars.

Case File: State v. Ramjema

Following the Chicken Wars, legal action was pursued against Ramjema regarding asset destruction (companion foxes, high-velocity equine). Ramjema represented himself against plaintiff counsel Backwards Rebel.

The verdict was decisively against Ramjema, resulting in a sentence of two weeks' confinement to the Skyblock dimension.
