TITLE ONE - LOW LEVEL CRIMES

§1.01 Theft

An act of unlawfully taking someone else's property with the intent to permanently deprive them of it. Punishment: 4 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.02 Intimidation

An act of making someone feel frightened, threatened, or coerced, often with the aim of influencing their behavior or decisions. This can involve verbal threats, physical actions, or other means of instilling fear. Punishment: 3 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.03 Harassing

An act of engaging in unwanted and distressing behavior with the intent to disturb, torment, or intimidate someone. Harassment can take various forms, including verbal or physical, and is often characterized by its repetitive nature. Punishment: 3 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs

§1.04 Vandalism

An act of intentional and unlawful destruction, defacement, or damage of public, government-owned assets. Punishment: 3 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.05 Reckless driving

An act of operating a vehicle with a blatant disregard for the safety of others, often involving dangerous behaviors such as excessive speeding, aggressive maneuvers, or other actions that pose a significant risk of harm. Punishment: 3 minutes of arrest, prohibition of driving any sort of a transport on the server; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.06 Complicity

Involvement or participation in a wrongful or illegal act, often as an accomplice or accessory, implying some level of collaboration or assistance in the commission of the offense. Punishment: 3 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.07 Improper disposal of state property

Unauthorized or inappropriate disposal or handling of assets or belongings owned by a government entity. This can include actions such as discarding, selling, or using state property for personal gain without proper authorization. Punishment: 5 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.08 Substandard workmanship

Performing work or production that is below the standards settled by the state. Punishment: Public humiliation, demotion; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs, Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

§1.09 Disturbance of public order

Actions or behaviors that disrupt the normal functioning and peace within a community or public space. Punishment: 4 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§1.10 Insubordination

An act of willfully disobeying or refusing to comply with a legitimate and lawful order or authority, typically within a hierarchical organization or workplace. Punishment: 3 minutes of arrest or demotion with one rank or, in harder cases, exile from the Communist Party; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

TITLE TWO - MIDDLE LEVEL CRIMES

§2.01 Illegal crossing of state borders

An act of entering or exiting the country without proper authorization. This can involve individuals bypassing designated border checkpoints, using unauthorized routes, or evading border control procedures. Punishment: 5 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Border Troops of KGB and the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.02 Unauthorized assembly

Gathering or meeting of individuals in a public space without the necessary permits or official authorization from relevant authorities. Punishment: 5 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.03 Failure to report for state service

An act of not showing up for duty or work obligations within a government or state organization as required. This can include employees or individuals who are expected to perform specific tasks, carry out assigned responsibilities, or attend work-related activities but do not fulfill these obligations. Punishment: Public humiliation, demotion; enforced by the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

§2.04 Slander against state officials

An act of making false and damaging spoken statements about individuals holding public offices or positions within the government. Punishment: Public humiliation, 3 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs and by the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

§2.05 Armed robbery

An act involving the use of force or threats, often accompanied by the possession of weapons, to unlawfully seize property. Punishment: 6 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.06 Blackmail

A criminal offense that involves threatening to reveal damaging, disgraceful, or sensitive information about a person unless they comply with certain demands. Punishment: Exile from all divisions and branches, penal transportation (time-out) for 5 days; enforced by the Committee for State Security.

§2.07 Failure to report Counter-revolutionary activities

A conscious act of failure to report to state authorities about actions against the state. Punishment: Exile and/or permanent appealable blacklist from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Committee for State Security, put on KGB watchlist for 1 month; enforced by the Committee for State Security.

§2.08 Attempted murder

A criminal offense that occurs when an individual takes substantial steps towards intentionally causing the death of another person but does not succeed in completing the act. Punishment: Arrest for 4 minutes; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.09 Complicity of murder

Involvement of an individual in the commission of a murder, either as an accomplice. Punishment: Arrest for 4 minutes; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.10 Manslaughter

A criminal offense involving the unlawful killing of another person, but by an accident. Punishment: Arrest for 4 minutes; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.11 Obstruction of state-sanctioned activities

An act of interference, hindrance, or disruption of government-approved or authorized actions, processes, events or functions. Punishment: Arrest for 3 minutes; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§2.12 Bourgeoise behavior

Attitudes, lifestyles, or actions associated with the bourgeoisie. Punishment: Exile and blacklist from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, blacklist from the Committee of State Security, public humiliation; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

§2.13 Trafficking

Illegal trade or movement of goods, people, or substances. Punishment: Arrest for 5 minutes; enforced by the Border Troops of KGB and the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

TITLE THREE - HIGH LEVEL CRIMES

§3.01 Murder

A criminal offense that involves the intentional killing of another person with malice aforethought. Punishment: 6 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§3.02 Assassination

Deliberate and targeted killing of a prominent or important person, often for political, ideological, or religious reasons. Punishment: 8 minutes of arrest; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§3.03 Hostage taking

An act where individuals are seized or held against their will, typically to compel a third party, such as a government or organization, to meet certain demands. Punishment: 6 minutes of arrest, if needed, liquidation; enforced by GRU Spetsnaz, Ministry of Internal Affairs.

§3.04 Corruption

Abuse of power or authority for personal gain, often involving unethical or illegal practices. It can manifest in various forms, including bribery, embezzlement, fraud, nepotism, extortion, or other dishonest activities. Punishment: Deportation to the capitalist countries (exile from the group), blacklist from all high-ranked positions and from all divisions including the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; enforced by the Committee of State Security.

§3.05 Anti-revolutionary rhetoric

Use of speech, propaganda, or communication that opposes and/or criticizes revolutionary ideas or spreading disinformation about the state and/or the government. Punishment: Deportation to the capitalist countries (exile from the group), blacklist from all high-ranked positions and from all divisions, blacklist and exile from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; enforced by the Committee of State Security.

§3.06 Bribing

Act of offering, giving, receiving, or soliciting something of value, often money, with the intent to influence the actions or decisions of an individual. Punishment: Penal transportation for 10 days, blacklist from all high ranked positions, prohibition to be elected, exile from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and blacklist from the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the Committee of State Security; enforced by the Committee of State Security.

§3.07 Exploitation

An act of using power to abuse someone or a group of people, or to get unfair advantage over him or them. Punishment: Demotion in all branches, exile from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, blacklist from the Committee of State Security, public humiliation; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the Committee of State Security, Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

§3.08 Racism

Supporting and proclaiming superiority of one race over another, leading to discrimination, and unequal treatment based on race, racial slurs. Punishment: Deportation to the capitalist countries (exile from the group), blacklist from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and law-enforcement divisions, public humiliation; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs, Committee of State Security, Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

§3.09 Gender discrimination (male/female)

An act treating individuals differently or unfairly based on their gender. Punishment: Demotion in all branches, exile and blacklist from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Committee of State Security; enforced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the Committee of State Security.

CAPITAL LEVEL CRIMES

C.1 Terrorism

Violent or destructive behavior that aims to create fear, panic, and intimidation within a population. Punishment: Liquidation; enforced by GRU Spetsnaz, Ministry of Internal Affairs, Committee of State Security, Border Troops of KGB.

C.2 Counter-revolutionary activities

Actions, strategies, or activities designed to undermine, destabilize, remove the Power of the People. Punishment: Declaration as an Enemy of the People following Public Execution (permanent ban and group-wide blacklist); enforced by the Committee of State Security.

C.3 Creation of an opposition group

Creation of an opposition group or organization that aims to overthrow or dramatically change the current political system. Punishment: All members of it are declared as Enemies of the People and they are being Publicly Executed (permanently banned and group-wde blacklisted); enforced by the Committee of State Security.

C.4 Espionage

Practice of obtaining or collecting information, typically of a confidential, or classified nature. Punishment: Public Execution (permanent ban and group-wide blacklist); enforced by the Committee of State Security.

C.5 Attempted Coup d'état

Attempt of Illegal seizure of political power by a group, typically carried out by the military or other elements within the government in order to restore capitalism. Punishment: Declaration of the members of the coup as Enemies of the People and Public Execution (permanent ban and group-wide blacklist); enforced by the Committee of State Security.

C.6 Betrayal of the cause of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolution

Actions committed by citizens of the USSR to the detriment of the military power of the USSR, its state independence, or the inviolability of its territory, such as: disclosure of military or state secrets, defection to the enemy, fleeing abroad. Punishment: Declaration as an Enemy of the People and Public Execution (permanent ban and group-wide blacklist); enforced by the Committee of State Security.
