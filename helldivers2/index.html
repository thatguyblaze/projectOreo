Stims: Peak Performance Enhancers

Standard-issue Stimulants (Stims) are a testament to Super Earth's commitment to Helldiver well-being and operational effectiveness. These medically approved injections provide rapid battlefield healing and stamina restoration, ensuring our soldiers can fight harder and longer for Democracy.

Rumors of addiction or side effects are baseless propaganda spread by dissidents and alien sympathizers. Stims are perfectly safe, non-addictive, and essential for mission success. Any Helldiver reporting adverse effects may require re-education.
